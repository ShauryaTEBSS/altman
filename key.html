The "Water Tank" Logic (Tricky!)
Problem: 240L left. 1/3 evaporated, 1/4 cleaned, 1/5 leaked. What was total?

Reverse Method (Working Backwards):

Think of it like rewinding a movie.

  1. The End (Evaporation): We have 240L left. The problem says 1/3 evaporated. That means 2/3 was left.
    If 2 parts = 240L, then 1 part = 120L.
    So before evaporation (3 parts) = 360 Litres.
  2. The Middle (Cleaning): We have 360L. The problem says 1/4 was used. That means 3/4 was left.
    If 3 parts = 360L, then 1 part = 120L.
    So before cleaning (4 parts) = 480 Litres.
  3. The Start (Leakage): We have 480L. The problem says 1/5 leaked. That means 4/5 was left.
    If 4 parts = 480L, then 1 part = 120L.
    So full tank (5 parts) = 600 Litres.

Final Answer: 600 Litres

SECTION F: MENTAL MANIA (Logic Traps)
1. The Train Trap
"Multiply that entire result by the smallest one-digit whole number."

It doesn't matter how many passengers or coins there are. The calculation chain ends with a multiplication.
The smallest one-digit whole number is 0.
Math Rule: Anything √ó 0 = 0.
Don't waste time calculating the passengers!
